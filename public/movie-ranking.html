The Best Movies according to Vikas

My top three movies of all time

The Prestige

This is my favourite movie. It's really complicated and has a great plot. It keeps the audience on the edge of thier seats all the time.

Guardians of Galaxy - Vol 1

This the best Marvel movie according to me. The humour element of this movie is simply outstanding. Great entertainment.

True Lies

If you are looking for wholesome entertainment, then this is the one. It has everything from action to emotion to comedy....One of the best movies ever made.
